The Launch Event

A Space for Connection

The ‘We See You’ campaign will culminate in an exciting Launch Event, bringing these themes of hybrid identity, community, and self-expression to life. The event will be a powerful mix of art, music, performance, and talks,  with each element echoing the diverse perspectives featured in the campaign. We believe that real change comes from meaningful connections, so the Launch Event is designed to be more than just an exhibition. It’s a space where conversations about identity, culture, and belonging can happen.

Experience the new cultural section of i-D – live. Location: Tolhuistuin, Amsterdam Date: - Time: 17:30 – 23:00 Free entry RSVP via QR

What’s happening?

This is more than an event. It’s a space for honest stories, raw energy, and unexpected connections. The evening includes: A visual exhibition featuring stills, quotes and identity in layers A panel talk with makers and creatives (feat. Ahmet Polat, Maru Asmellash and more) DJ sets, performances & spontaneous moments in the garden A bar, music, and space to hang, reflect and meet each other

Why come?

To be part of something real. To hear the voices you don’t always hear. To see yourself in the pages of i-D. This launch marks the beginning of a new direction where hybrid identity is no longer an exception, but part of the whole. Come early, stay late, bring a friend and step into the world of the new i-D.   We See You.

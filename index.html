Embrace Your Hybrid Identity.

At i-D, we believe in the power of identity that cannot be boxed into one category. Our newly revamped Multi i-D cultural section is committed to providing a platform for those who live between worlds, people whose identities are shaped by multiple cultures, languages, and experiences. We See You is not just a campaign; it’s a celebration of the complexity and richness of hybrid identities.

This campaign is the beginning of something much bigger.  We are giving the space to those voices and stories that haven’t been heard, creating a community where belonging is about embracing complexity, not fitting into a predefined mold.
